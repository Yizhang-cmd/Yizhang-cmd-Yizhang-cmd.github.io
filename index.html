About Me

Hi, I'm Yizhang Zhu, a third-year Interactive Arts and Technology (IAT) student at SFU. My studies have led me through various creative projects, from brand book design and storyboarding to sound editing. Currently, I’m working on projects in 3D modeling and animation.

Music also plays a big role in my life, with Muse, Daft Punk, Gorillaz, and Twenty One Pilots being a few of my go-to bands.
